Our Story

The origins of Xpose Market trace back to 2015, beginning with the launch of a single social media page. During the early boom of social media, the founder discovered a passion for digital marketing. What started as an Instagram account sharing humorous videos and images quickly gained momentum, attracting nearly 20,000 followers within just six months. By actively engaging with the community and applying emerging sales and promotional strategies, the audience expanded to more than 150,000 followers across multiple platforms within a year and a half.

As brand inquiries from clothing labels and retail businesses began to replace personal requests, the founder recognized a shift in the marketplace. Motivated by the support of the local community, the focus turned toward finding effective ways to help local businesses and brands broaden their reach.

Drawing from professional experience in the restaurant industry and hospitality sales, the founder witnessed firsthand the ability of digital marketing to drive measurable growth—from neighborhood shops to established clothing brands. This journey laid the foundation for Xpose Market’s mission: empowering businesses through strategic, creative, and results-driven marketing.
